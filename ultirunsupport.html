Welcome, this is the support page for the Ultirun mobile app on IOS and Android devices!

Enter the wondrous world of the Superverse, a far future world of alternate worlds
where people drive in flying vehicles known as Pods and many even have superpowers who are
classified as Exhumans, but some of them don't use it for good. In fact, an infamous cult of
Exhumans known as the Novi Angeli have come to terrorize the traffic in the city of Versepolis,
attacking pods and the people in them! Play as Meditat Viatorem, the hero of Versepolis, and use your
high tech supersuit and powers of creation to parkour and fight to fend off the Novi Angeli in an infinite
action platformer that interweaves a complex parkour and combat system to make you the ultimate warrior.
